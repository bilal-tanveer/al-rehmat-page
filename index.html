Welcome to World of Technical Knowledge

AL-REHMAT is a leading IT training institute in Rahim Yar Khan, Pakistan, established by Hello World Technologies. We offer a comprehensive range of training programs, including Full Stack Web Development, App Development, Graphic Designing, WordPress Development, Digital Marketing, Software Quality Assurance, UI/UX Design, Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Front-end Development, Business Development, Social Media Content Creation, Content Writing, Project Management, and React JS.

Our courses are designed to provide students with practical learning experiences that equip them with the skills necessary to succeed in the rapidly evolving technology industry. We have experienced trainers who provide individual attention to students, guiding them to develop their technical skills, creativity, and problem-solving abilities. At ICR, we are committed to providing a one-stop-shop for students to acquire various skills under one roof, enabling them to become bread earners without any difficulty. Our mission is to empower our students to achieve their career goals and become successful IT professionals.
